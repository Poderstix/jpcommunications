About JPCInc.®

Built on Honesty, Integrity & Professionalism

JP Communications Inc. conducts all business based on the principle of HIP — Honesty, Integrity and Professionalism. Our primary goal as a leading online B2B global trade platform is to provide superior service to all our customers, wholesale buyers, and suppliers.

Founded in 2005 by CEO Jason Prescott and former CTO Joseph Preston, JPCInc. launched as a network of B2B global trade platforms. The flagship site, the wholesale search engine TopTenWholesale.com, launched that same year.

JPCInc. takes pride in its ability to solve industry problems. Our innovations enable stakeholders to connect globally for mutually profitable trade transactions, use free community resources for gaining industry knowledge, and access cutting-edge sourcing tools.

We strive to serve our customers' best interests while maintaining an exceptional reputation among industry stakeholders. Our forward-thinking philosophy and passion for helping others "Find it. Source it. Profit!" on the internet have served us well.

Our Services

Complete Supply-Chain Solutions

JPCInc.® offers a diverse range of services designed to solve real industry problems and connect buyers with the right suppliers efficiently.

🛡️

Supplier Pass™ Verification

Premium members are suppliers and service providers who have successfully passed a Company Profile Verification (CPV) by an accredited third-party agency. The most trusted supplier verification system in B2B trade.

🎯

Custom Sourcing

Our specialists match buyers' detailed sourcing requirements with the right suppliers — efficiently and at scale. Post a detailed RFQ and connect with pre-qualified Premium and Verified suppliers. Save time. Save costs. Find quality.

🚚

Logistics & Warehousing

Warehousing facilities strategically located in Los Angeles and New Jersey — the most active ports in America. We also offer packaging, assembly, shrink wrapping, and other value-added services for eCommerce and manufacturing clients.

📊

Advertising Solutions

A wide range of advertising products enabling suppliers to reach thousands of wholesale buyers monthly. Pay Per Click, banner advertising, hot buy buttons, and seller preview video ads available across both platforms.

🎬

Wholesale Video Hub

JPCInc.® launched the first dedicated video hub where the wholesale community can view and post business videos. Suppliers showcase their factories, products, and capabilities to buyers worldwide — free of charge.

🤝

Trade Show Solutions

Add trade shows to your company profile and connect with serious buyers attending events worldwide. Choose from hundreds of international and domestic trade shows — and let buyers know which ones you'll be attending.

Meet the Founder

JP Communications Inc. was built by one of the most experienced figures in the global wholesale, manufacturing, and retail sourcing industry.

JP
Jason Aaron Prescott
Founder & CEO — JP Communications Inc. (JPCInc.®)

Jason Prescott founded JP Communications Inc. in 2005 with a vision to facilitate international trade at every level. He launched TopTenWholesale.com that same year, and in 2009 acquired Manufacturer.com from Hangzhou, China — expanding JPC's reach to global factory-grade suppliers. That year, Jason was also named Trade Ambassador by the Chinese government's Jiaozhou Investment Promotion Bureau.

He is the author of Wholesale 101 and co-author of Retail 101 with Nicole Leinbach Reyhle. He is also the founder of the Apparel Textile Sourcing trade shows — now the fastest-growing shows in the USA, Canada, and Europe — and founder of Alteca Home Builders, a residential construction company based in South Fork, Colorado.
